# FinVerse - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Vision

FinVerse is a production-grade personal finance platform that helps users manage every aspect of their financial life through a secure, modern, and intelligent web application.

The goal is to demonstrate professional software engineering practices including backend development, testing, automation, DevOps, and AI integration.

---

## Target Users

- Students
- Working professionals
- Freelancers
- Families
- Small business owners

---

## Primary Goals

- Track income and expenses
- Manage multiple wallets and bank accounts
- Create monthly budgets
- Monitor savings goals
- Track investments
- Generate reports
- Provide AI-powered financial insights
